PhenoMeNal H2020 wiki

PhenoMeNal provides Virtual Research Environments (VRE) for interoperable and scalable metabolomics analysis. This wiki holds information pertaining to its usage and development.

Resources for Users

The PhenoMeNal infrastructure will be available for deployment in public and private clouds, as well as local hardware. The main access for users will be through our Virtual Research Environment (VRE).
