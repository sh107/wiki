Fluxomics tutorial

The science behind 13C traced MS fluxomics data analyisis is explained on this video.

To try the workflow, please download and unzip first this data to your own machine (click on the download button to the upper right of your browser window). Once you have that data unzipped, please follow this video tutorial "Fluxomics workflow demo" from a fresh VRE deployment, which will explain how to use the different pieces of data on the workflow.
